"We Know Game" About Our Franchise

Champs Sports, a distinguished member of the Foot Locker family, stands as a premier retailer committed to empowering athletes and sport enthusiasts with an exceptional selection of quality sports apparel, footwear, and accessories. Since our foundation in 1984, we have strived to not only provide the latest and best in athletic gear but also to embody a profound commitment to the communities we are a part of. As an integral branch of the Foot Locker, Inc. conglomerate, Champs Sports leverages our extensive network and resources to launch and support a variety of community outreach programs and events, aiming to inspire, motivate, and uplift individuals in their athletic pursuits and beyond. Our dedication to making a positive impact is evident in our efforts to unite and support the neighborhoods that welcome us, reinforcing our belief in the transformative power of sports. At Champs Sports, we are more than just a store; we are a community partner dedicated to making a meaningful difference, one step at a time.

At Champs Sports, we guarantee an exceptional selection of the latest athletic apparel, footwear, and accessories, ensuring you're always at the top of your game and style. Every purchase comes with the guarantee of contributing to community empowerment, offering you a shopping experience that blends quality, passion, and a commitment to making a difference. Choose Champs Sports for a guarantee of satisfaction and connection to a broader community of sports enthusiasts.
